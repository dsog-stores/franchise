The DSOG Franchise Workflow

Our structured process ensures your success from day one

1

Application & Approval

Submit your franchise application. Our team reviews your suitability and provides initial consultation to ensure mutual alignment.

2

Training & Setup

Complete comprehensive training on our systems, products, and customer service. We help you set up your retail space with our branding.

3

Inventory Supply

Receive your initial inventory from our trusted suppliers. We maintain strict quality control across all products from brands like Visha Apparel and Yarnxe.

4

Grand Opening

Launch your DSOG franchise with marketing support from our team. We provide promotional materials and launch strategies.

5

Ongoing Support

Receive continuous support including inventory management, marketing assistance, and business development guidance from our experienced team.

6

Delivery System

Utilize our proprietary DSOG Delivery System to ship products to your customers across different locations with reliable logistics support.
